What Is This?

This is an active work-in-progress. I just wanted to build myself a sandbox to learn some more about the exciing new HTML5 <canvas> element. I will try never to remove any functionality as I hack away at it, though I can't envision any scenario when you really need to trip out to some pretty colours (not that it's not great fun).

I will one day blog about all this. Until then, the rest of my blog is here.

The Source

I'll be the first to admit that this isn't the most innovative app ever. What it is is a neat little example of what you can do with a couple of loops and the most basic knowledge about the HTML5 <canvas> element (and trusty Javascript).
